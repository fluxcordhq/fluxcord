Refund & Policy

Refund Policy

At Fluxcord, we strive to provide the best service possible. Due to the digital nature of our products, refunds are handled on a case-by-case basis.

Eligible for Refund:

  • Product not delivered within 24 hours of purchase
  • Service does not work as described
  • Technical issues on our end preventing delivery
  • Duplicate purchases made by mistake

Not Eligible for Refund:

  • Change of mind after product delivery
  • User error or misuse of the product
  • Discord account banned or suspended (not our responsibility)
  • Products already used or activated

Refund requests must be submitted within 48 hours of purchase through our Discord support channel.

Terms of Service

1. Service Usage

By purchasing from Fluxcord, you agree to use our services in accordance with Discord's Terms of Service. We are not responsible for any consequences resulting from Discord policy violations.

2. Product Delivery

Most products are delivered automatically within minutes. Some services may take up to 24 hours. Delivery times may vary based on demand and Discord API limitations.

3. Account Security

Never share your account credentials with anyone. Fluxcord will never ask for your Discord password. We only require necessary permissions to deliver services.

4. Payment Processing

All payments are processed securely through our payment provider. We accept cryptocurrency and other digital payment methods. Prices are subject to change without notice.

5. Support & Disputes

For any issues, contact our support team via Discord. We aim to respond within 12 hours. Disputes should be raised within 48 hours of purchase.

6. Service Limitations

We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone for any reason. Products are provided "as-is" without warranties. We are not liable for Discord policy changes that affect our services.

Privacy Policy

Fluxcord respects your privacy. We collect minimal information necessary to provide our services:

  • Discord User ID and Server ID for service delivery
  • Payment information (processed securely by third-party providers)
  • Order history for support and refund purposes

We do not sell or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is used solely for service delivery and customer support.
